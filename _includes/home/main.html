What I love & What I do

I'm a graphic designer, front-end developer and tech writer. I've been a graphic design student at HAN University of Applied Sciences and I graduated in February, 2019.

I love typographic design, 3D modeling, photography, making videos, motion graphics and doing creative things with graphics. In addition to that I'm also interested in web development, setting up Linux servers & creating games in Unity.

Knowing that I both love graphic design and tech stuff, I chose to become a front-end developer. A front-end developer builds websites according to a design given by a web designer. It's exactly on the edge of design and tech.

I write about the things I love and do and you read about it on my blog.
